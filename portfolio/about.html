About Me

Born and raised in uptown New Orleans, LA, my outlook on life and work is both eclectic and passionate, much like my city.

After a college summer internship on Capitol Hill, I caught the DC bug and moved to our nation's Capitol three weeks after graduating from the University of Georgia (c/o 2011). In my 2.5 years in DC, I worked at Burson-Marsteller and The Glover Park Group, prominent public affairs firms. In fall 2013, I moved home to New Orleans to pursue a Project Management position with Zehnder Communications. After five months in this role, I was recruited into the Account Service team in March 2014. As an Account Coordinator I was able to hone my strategy and writing skills, while building relationships with banking, health care and lifestyle clients; an ideal role for me since I weirdly don't hate small talk.

In February 2015, I found myself in a position to pause my advertising career to pursue other passions: yoga and travel. I completed a 200 hour yoga teacher certification in April 2015 and two weeks later flew to Myanmar to kick off four months of backpacking in Southeast Asia and Australia. Back home in New Orleans, I am now opening my job search to advertising and marketing roles in New Orleans, San Francisco and Sydney with emphasis on digital and travel. I hope to bring my unique background in client service, project management and public relations plus my travel experiences to a challenging and strategic position.
